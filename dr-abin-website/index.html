Specialist Orthodontist

Dr. Abin Mathew B.D.S., M.D.S.

Welcome to my practice. I believe that a smile is more than just a set of teeth—it is your most powerful form of self-expression. I am Dr. Abin Mathew, a Specialist Orthodontist with over 8 years of clinical experience across India, Saudi Arabia, and the UAE, dedicated to blending clinical precision with an artistic eye to create smiles that are as healthy as they are transformative.

With a Master of Dental Surgery (M.D.S.) in Orthodontics, I have spent my career mastering the delicate balance of dental aesthetics and structural alignment. My international experience has allowed me to treat a diverse range of patients, and I am proud to offer care in English, Hindi, Malayalam, and Tamil, while maintaining a strong working proficiency in Arabic to ensure every patient feels comfortable and heard. My approach is rooted in the belief that orthodontic treatment is a collaborative journey; my goal is to guide you through that process with clarity, comfort, and the highest standard of care.

My Philosophy

I treat every patient with the same dedication I apply to my own life. I bring a high level of focus, precision, and drive to your orthodontic treatment plan. I don’t just move teeth; I craft confidence.

At the core of my practice is a commitment to:

  • Evidence-Based Excellence: Utilizing the latest advancements in orthodontic technology to ensure predictable, long-term results.
  • Personalized Care: Recognizing that every smile is unique, I develop bespoke treatment plans tailored to your specific facial anatomy and lifestyle goals.
  • Comfort & Clarity: I prioritize creating a welcoming environment where you feel empowered, informed, and confident in every step of your journey.

Why Choose My Practice?

Choosing an orthodontist is a significant decision. When you step into my clinic, you are choosing a partner who values your time, your comfort, and your results. Whether you are seeking discreet alignment options or comprehensive orthodontic care, I am here to provide a sophisticated, modern experience that yields exceptional outcomes.

Featured Transformations

Real cases demonstrating our commitment to structural alignment and aesthetic perfection.

Impacted Canines

Complex disimpaction utilizing Diode Lasers and Damon Q Self-Ligating Brackets to perfectly align teeth into the arch.

Cleft Palate Correction

Specialized multidisciplinary care utilizing NiTi Palatal Expanders and MBT mechanotherapy for transformative results.

Bimaxillary Protrusion

Absolute anchorage cases leveraging 1.5 x 9 mm titanium implants for precise and controlled retraction.

Myofunctional Therapy

Facemask and Twin Block appliances for Class III and Class II skeletal modifications in growing patients.

Deep Bite Correction

Strategic bite opening using advanced Damon systems and infrazygomatic crest implants for stable anchorage.

Orthognathic Surgery

Pre-surgical orthodontics for LeFort 1 Osteotomy, ensuring perfect alignment before and after jaw surgery.
